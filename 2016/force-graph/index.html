This chart visualises part of the Majestic backlink database.

Starting with the site of the university that you choose in the dropdown above, this shows the main backlinks pointing to the site, and the backlinks to these neighbouring sites and so on.

As links in HTML can only go one way, the only way to obtain this information on a web scale is to crawl the world-wide-web and invert the entire linking structure using networks of powerful computers.

Majestic has been a pioneer in commercial backlink intelligence and has constructed the world's largest map of it's type.

Left-click and drag to pin a node and display it's information. Double-click a node to unpin it.

Right-click to visit the site that a node represents.

Nodes are coloured by their main Topic, and links are coloured based on the Trust Flow of the backlinking site.
